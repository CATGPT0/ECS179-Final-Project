## About the Game ##
Blade and Card: Secret of the Land is a combination of a traditional 2D top-down role-playing game and a card game. The story takes place in a magic world that has been invaded by monsters. You, as the player, must travel through the world to defeat the monsters in your world and find the secret of the world. Throughout this journey, you will run into friendly NPCs and monsters. You can interact with the NPCs and play a card game against them. As for the monsters, you must protect yourself and fight against them to survive. Defeat the monsters to level up, explore the land to collect objects, and find the secret of the world!

**Release Date**: 3/21/2024  
**Price**: Free

## Brief Overview/Features ##
- Explore the magic world. Includes grasslands, a village, an arena, a lake, an abandoned village, a snow field, and an ice field.
- Find hidden treasure chests located on the map
- Open treasure chests and collect powerups and weapons
- Escape and fight against monsters to gain xp and level up
- Meet NPCs throughout the world and participate in a card battle that can help you gain advantages in future combat

**Future Features**
- Boss monsters appearing upon reaching certain levels
